About

I am a Full-Stack Developer with a Mechanical Engineering background and a passion for crafting innovative technological solutions. My expertise includes web development, data visualization, and system integrations. Proficient in modern frameworks and technologies such as Node.js, React, Python, and MongoDB, I delivered data-driven insights using BI tools like Power BI and Looker. Throughout my career, I’ve led innovative projects, from designing Management Information Systems (MIS) for large-scale e-commerce operations to developing monitoring platforms and robust data workflows. My professional journey includes key roles at Supercampo, enhancing the digital presence of agricultural cooperatives, and SIMEPAR, contributing to critical dam monitoring system. Additionally, I’ve modernized back-end systems for BRG Brasil Geradores and developed automation tools and full-stack applications as a freelancer. My main achievements include being recognized as a LIF-7 Fellow by the Royal Academy of Engineering (UK), winning multiple hackathons, and contributing to award-winning sustainable entrepreneurship project. I am committed to lifelong learning, considering my training in full-stack web development, data engineering, and data science.
